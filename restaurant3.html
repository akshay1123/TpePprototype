Ohri's Jiva Imperia
Review 1:
The buffet here is so-so. The service seems to declines every time I go there. The best part of their buffet are the assorted salads . it has a very good ambience and is mostly recommended for family outings or get togethers!
Review 2:
This is the place to be for any Vegan. The wide spread along with its good quality of food sets up your stomach for a treat. The lights and the ambiance are simply superb. The spread covers many cuisines like Chinese, north Indian, South Indian, Gujarati etc. The desserts spread particularly ice creams are too good as there are more variants. And not to forget about the chocolate fountain. The best part is the price at which it is offered.
Review 3:
Perfect place to find a wide variety of cuisines under one roof. Loved the ambience. Another hit by the Ohris family. Loved the food. A big plus is the large desert selection.
